Overview
========

Welcome to the India Learning Patnership (ILP) - Partner's Handbook. The objective of this handbook is to provide information on setting up a fully functional ILP instance.

Audience
--------

ILP is targeted at:

* All non-profit organizations in the education sector
* All state government education departments
* Volunteers, community, and parents
